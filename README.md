## Task 1 | Camera calibration
### Basic:
- Familiarize with camera calibration method
- Take pictures with given camera
- Calibrate camera with taken pictures
- Calculate calibration metrics
- Make realtime image correction
- Show solution to instructor

### Advanced:
- Implement DLT or Zhang's method
- Take into account distortion or not
